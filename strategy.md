# Trade Copier Strategy & Configuration Guide

## 1. Overall Copying Strategy
This application acts as an automated trade copier that bridges Telegram signals with a MetaTrader 5 (MT5) broker account. 

### How It Works:
1. **Signal Parsing**: It continuously listens to a connected Telegram channel for messages matching a specific text footprint: `(OPEN|CLOSE) (BUY|SELL) {SYMBOL} {PRICE} {LOT}LOT ... ORDER ID:{ID}`.
2. **Symbol Resolution**: When an `OPEN` signal arrives, the backend searches your MT5 broker's symbol list. It handles broker-specific suffix extensions (like `.m` or `.pro`) automatically so that signals like `EURUSD` safely map to `EURUSD.pro`.
3. **Smart Execution**: Trades are executed directly on MT5 as Immediate-or-Cancel (`IOC`) with Good-Till-Cancelled (`GTC`) timing and a deviation of `20` ticks to allow for slight slippage.
4. **Closing Trades**: When a `CLOSE` signal containing a matching `ORDER ID` comes in, the system locates the open MT5 position generated by that signal and fires a market closing order.

## 2. Dynamic Lot Sizing & Money Management
Instead of blindly copying the signal provider's exact lot size, this copier uses a **Proportional Auto-Scaling** strategy based on your account's equity.

### The Math:
`Executed Lot = (Current MT5 Account Balance / Base Balance) * Signal Lot Size`

**Example:**
- Provider Signal Lot: `0.5 Lot`
- `base_balance`: `$1,000` (Your benchmark configuration)
- Current Account Balance: `$2,000`
- Resulting Executed Lot: `(2000 / 1000) * 0.5 = 1.0 Lot`

*This dynamic scaling ensures your risk incrementally grows or shrinks dynamically as your account balance changes over time.*

## 3. Frontend Settings Parameter Definitions

All copying configurations can be altered directly from the React Frontend Dashboard **Settings** page. Below is a detailed breakdown of what each parameter does and how it affects the trading strategy:

### `allow_trading`
- **Type:** Boolean (`true` / `false` Toggle)
- **Description:** Acts as a master kill switch. When turned off (`false`), the listener will still parse incoming Telegram signals, but it **will not** pass them to the MT5 executor. Use this to quickly pause automated trading during unpredictable high-impact news events or weekend maintenance without shutting down the core app. 

### `base_balance`
- **Type:** Number (Float)
- **Default:** `1000.0`
- **Description:** The foundational metric for the Proportional Auto-Scaling math. It represents the hypothetical account balance upon which the provider bases their standard lot sizes. 
  - **Lowering** this value *increases* your risk (results in larger executed lots).
  - **Raising** this value *decreases* your risk (results in smaller executed lots).

### `min_balance_guard`
- **Type:** Number (Float)
- **Default:** `200.0`
- **Description:** A severe equity protection mechanism. Before executing any `OPEN` order, the system checks your live MT5 balance. If your balance dips below this numerical threshold, the calculated lot size is forced to `0.0` and the trade is **safely rejected**. This saves your account from a total margin call.

### `max_lot`
- **Type:** Number (Float)
- **Default:** `1.0`
- **Description:** The absolute ceiling for position risk. Even if your account balance scales a trade mathematically to an exceptionally large lot size, the originally executed volume will never exceed this `max_lot` value. This limits catastrophic overexposure from rogue signals or sudden massive balance spikes.

### `magic_number`
- **Type:** Number (Integer)
- **Default:** `202512`
- **Description:** A unique identifier integer assigned to every MT5 order placed by this specific software. MT5 uses magic numbers to differentiate between trades opened manually, trades opened by other Expert Advisors (EAs), and trades opened by this trade copier. It is heavily utilized behind the scenes when finding the correct matching positions to `CLOSE`.

### `telegram_group_link`
- **Type:** URL String 
- **Description:** Stores the invite link or URL of the Telegram source group you are scraping signals from. This is largely used as an informational text reference inside the UI dashboard.

## 4. Key Performance Metrics to Track

To properly evaluate the health and safety of the copying strategy, closely monitor these three critical risk factors:

### 1. 🔴 Max Drawdown
What’s the deepest negative? 
(-10%? -30%? -70%?)

### 2. ⚠️ Margin Level
Does it go close to stop-out? 
This is how accounts die.

### 3. ⏳ Trade Duration
How long do losing trades stay open? 
Hours? Days?
